Tile & Grout Cleaning Service

At 4EVA Kleen, we use a high‑pressure, high‑heat, and vacuum recovery tile & grout cleaning system designed to restore floors to their original condition. Our industry‑grade detergents combined with advanced rinsing technology ensure deep cleaning that removes dirt, grease, and bacteria from grout lines, leaving your tiles fresh, hygienic, and shining again.

How our professional tile and grout cleaning works:

  • We begin by applying a specially formulated cleaning agent to loosen embedded dirt.
  • Next, we power scrub the tile and grout surfaces to agitate and lift contaminants.
  • Finally, we perform a high‑pressure hot water rinse and extraction, leaving the floor clean, dry, and ready to walk on immediately.
